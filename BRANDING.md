Symi – Branding Guide

🧠 Brand Essence

Symi is a calm, modern companion for people living with migraine.

Core idea:

Helping you understand patterns and create more good days.

⸻

🎯 Positioning

We are NOT:

* a clinical tool
* a cold medical tracker
* symptom-focused only

We ARE:

* calm
* supportive
* modern
* premium
* empowering

⸻

🗣️ Tone of Voice

Do:

* “Understand your patterns”
* “More good days”
* “A small step forward”

Avoid:

* “Track symptoms”
* “Analyze data”
* “Medical language”

⸻

🎨 Color System

Primary (Petrol): #0F3D3E
Primary Light: #1F5C5E

Secondary (Sage): #8ECDB8
Secondary Light: #CFEAE2

Accent (Coral): #FF8A7A
Accent Soft: #FFD1C9

Background: #F6F4EF
Card Background: #FFFFFF

Text Primary: #1C1C1E
Text Secondary: #6B6B6E

⸻

🔤 Typography

Primary Font: SF Pro (iOS)

Hierarchy:

* Title: 28–34pt / Semibold
* Subtitle: 18–22pt / Medium
* Body: 15–17pt / Regular
* Caption: 12–13pt / Regular

Guidelines:

* generous spacing
* avoid dense layouts
* prioritize readability

⸻

🔷 Logo & Icon

App Icon Principles

* 1024x1024
* full-bleed (no border, no padding)
* background reaches edges
* all elements inside iOS squircle

Visual Concept

* minimal journal shape
* soft flowing wave lines
* subtle depth
* calm gradient background (petrol → teal)

Avoid

* red colors
* medical symbols
* head / pain illustrations

⸻

🧩 UI Design Principles

Layout

* lots of whitespace
* soft cards (radius 16–20pt)
* subtle shadows

Components

Buttons

* Primary: Coral background, white text
* Secondary: Sage / outline

Cards

* rounded
* airy

Inputs

* minimal borders
* soft background fields

⸻

🌊 Visual Language

* soft waves (pattern = symptom flow)
* rounded shapes
* subtle gradients
* no sharp edges

⸻

🛍️ App Store Strategy

App Name

Symi – Migraine Journal

Screenshot Flow

1. Emotion → “More good days”
2. Simplicity → “Log in seconds”
3. Insight → “Find your patterns”
4. Control → “Everything in one place”
5. Trust → “Your data stays yours”

⸻

🧠 UX Principles

* reduce friction
* celebrate small wins
* avoid overwhelming users
* always feel supportive

⸻

🎯 Differentiation

Inspired by:

* Headspace (calm)
* Apple Health (clarity)

But focused on migraine understanding.

⸻

🚀 Future Extensions

* insights / AI recommendations
* Apple Health integration
* doctor reports

⸻

✅ Summary

Symi is:

* calm
* premium
* minimal
* supportive

Goal:

Help users create more good days with less effort.
